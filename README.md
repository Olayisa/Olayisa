> ## 🔬 Featured Project: Process Optimization & Downtime Analysis
>
> > **"Using IBM Maximo maintenance data, Prolink slurry density calibration records, and Serva Engineering Analytics, I identified that density deviations beyond ±0.5 ppg are the primary driver of oil well downtime — and demonstrated how certified calibration reduces operational losses."**
> >
> > **Key findings:**
> > - Uncalibrated slurry → high downtime & well integrity risk
> > - - Certified density (±0.2 ppg) → stable operations & efficiency gains
> >   - - Random Forest model predicts downtime from operational parameters
